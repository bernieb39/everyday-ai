Questions owners actually ask

Will this mean new software to learn?

No — that's the promise. I set AI up around the tools you already use: your phone, your WhatsApp, your inbox, the notebook by the till. Most "annoying task" problems don't need custom software at all, just the right AI flow set up well — and I sit with you until it's running on your real work. When something genuinely is worth building, I'll build it as a One-Shot Build and say so upfront.

Why is it free? What's the catch?

No catch — just timing. I'm at the start of this venture, and before I charge anyone I want a site full of real workflows optimized for real businesses. You get hours of your week back; I get a great example to show here (anonymized, and only with your OK). When I do start charging, early users keep what they have.

I barely have time to make payroll. How much of mine does this take?

One sit-down: 60–90 minutes, at your place, during your normal workday — you keep working, I watch. After that, the time flows back: the whole point is that the task runs without you. If prep, homework, or "requirements meetings" ever appear, I've done it wrong.

Is my business data safe?

Your customers, prices and numbers aren't shared, sold, or mixed with anyone else's, and wherever possible the flow runs on your own accounts. Anything sensitive — client lists, finances, contracts — we talk through explicitly before I touch it.

What happens when something breaks?

You tell me and I fix it — free, like everything else right now. Flows on Build & Care I monitor myself, so most problems are fixed before you notice them. And nothing I set up locks you in: stop anytime, and everything keeps belonging to you.
